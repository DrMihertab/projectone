Patient complaint score (PCS) and patient complaint report (PCR)

Project leader | September 2021- February 2022

Patient complaint score is an objective tool to assess the complaint burden of departments and the hospital using four dimensions of complaints. The tool is simple to compute and easily reproducible. It has been validated against quarterly patient satisfaction surveys, demonstrating a correlation with patient satisfaction levels. The report has enabled the hospital to prioritize areas for improvement and allowed departments to monitor their progress effectively.
